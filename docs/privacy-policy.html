Privacy Policy

BritPrep: UK Citizenship Test

Effective date: 16 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Yacer Abdelaziz handles information for the BritPrep: UK Citizenship Test app.

Data Collection

BritPrep: UK Citizenship Test does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, camera data, microphone data, or payment card details. The app does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or cross-app tracking.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores study data locally on your device, including:

  • question IDs you have answered and whether each answer was correct;
  • bookmarked question IDs;
  • mock test scores, pass/fail results, dates, and duration;
  • study streak and last active date;
  • whether the lifetime mock test unlock is active on the device.

This data is used only for app features such as progress tracking, bookmarks, mock test history, and premium access. It is not sent to us.

Purchases

BritPrep: UK Citizenship Test offers a one-time in-app purchase for lifetime access to mock tests. Purchases and restore-purchase checks are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Support

If you email us for support, we will use your email address and message only to respond to your request.

Retention

Study progress remains on your device until you reset progress, uninstall the app, or the operating system removes the data. Purchase records are handled by Apple according to Apple's policies.

Your Choices

  • You can reset app progress from the app settings.
  • You can delete local app data by uninstalling the app.
  • You can restore purchases through the app's restore purchase button.
  • You can manage App Store purchases, refunds, and account settings through Apple.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app or our practices change. The latest version will be posted on this page.
