Privacy Policy

UK LIFE TEST

Effective date: 10 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Yacer Abdelaziz ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the UK LIFE TEST mobile app and any related legal pages we publish for the app.

Summary

UK LIFE TEST is designed to work without user accounts, advertising, third-party analytics, tracking, location access, camera access, microphone access, contacts access, or a remote user database. The app stores your study progress on your own device so the app can remember your answers, bookmarks, mock test history, streak, and premium unlock status.

Information We Do Not Collect

Based on the current app design, we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, postal address, precise location, photos, contacts, messages, government identifiers, or payment card details. We do not sell personal information, and we do not use your activity for cross-app or cross-site tracking.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store the following information locally on your device using iOS device storage:

  • question IDs you have answered and whether each answer was correct;
  • bookmarked question IDs;
  • mock test scores, pass/fail results, dates, and duration;
  • study streak and last active date;
  • whether the lifetime mock test unlock is active on the device.

This information is used only to provide app features such as progress tracking, saved practice, score history, reset progress, and access to unlocked mock tests. This local study data is not sent to us.

Purchases

The app offers a one-time in-app purchase for lifetime access to mock tests. Purchases and restore-purchase checks are processed by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. Apple may process payment, account, tax, refund, and transaction information under Apple's own terms and privacy policies. We do not receive your payment card details.

The app only stores a local entitlement flag showing whether premium mock tests are unlocked.

Diagnostics And App Store Data

We do not add our own analytics or third-party crash-reporting SDK to the current native app. Apple may provide developers with aggregated App Store analytics, sales reports, or diagnostics depending on user settings and Apple's developer tools. Any such information is handled by Apple under Apple's policies and is not used by us to track you across apps or websites.

Web Version Or Demo Pages

If you use a web demo or web version of the app, study progress may be stored in your browser's local storage. Browser local storage stays on that browser unless you clear it, reset the app, or the browser removes it. A web hosting provider, such as GitHub Pages, may process basic server logs to deliver the legal pages or website.

How We Use Information

Local app data is used to operate app features on your device. If you contact us for support, we will use the information you choose to send, such as your email address and message, only to respond to your request and keep reasonable support records.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only if required by law, to respond to lawful requests, to protect our rights, or with service providers that help us operate the app or support users and are required to handle information appropriately.

Retention

Study progress stored on your device remains there until you reset progress, uninstall the app, clear the relevant device or browser storage, or the operating system removes it. Purchase records are retained by Apple according to Apple's policies. Support emails are retained only as long as reasonably needed for support, legal, and record-keeping purposes.

Your Choices

  • You can reset app progress from the app settings.
  • You can delete local app data by uninstalling the app.
  • You can restore purchases through the app's restore purchase button.
  • You can manage App Store purchases, refunds, and account settings through Apple.
  • You can contact us about privacy questions at abdelazizyacer@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended as a study companion for people preparing for the Life in the UK test. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

The app may be available to users in different countries. Because the current app does not send study progress to us, your study progress remains on your device. If you contact us, your message may be processed in the country where we or our support tools operate.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes, when legal requirements change, or when our data practices change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
